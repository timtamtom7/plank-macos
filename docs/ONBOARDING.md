# Plank — Onboarding

4 screens introducing the bookmark manager concept.

---

## Screen 1 — Welcome to Plank

**Headline:** Your bookmarks, finally organized.

**Body:** Plank keeps all your saved links in one beautiful, searchable library — synced across browsers, tagged, and ready when you need them.

**Illustration:** A warm, flat illustration of an open wooden planner/bookmark board with 3 colorful bookmark tabs visible. A single bookmark is mid-hover, about to be clipped on. Soft shadows. On a light cream background (`#FEFCE8`).

**CTA Button:** "Get Started →"

**Skip link:** "Skip setup" (bottom right, subtle)

---

## Screen 2 — Import Your Bookmarks

**Headline:** Import in one click.

**Body:** Plank automatically pulls bookmarks from Safari, Chrome, and Firefox — or import from your existing HTML bookmark files.

**Illustration:** A flat illustration showing browser icons (Safari, Chrome, Firefox) with arrows converging toward a central bookmark icon inside a circle. Each arrow in the app's accent blue. Clean, minimal, tech-forward.

**CTA Button:** "Choose Browsers…"

---

## Screen 3 — Organize with Tags & Folders

**Headline:** Find anything in seconds.

**Body:** Create folders for projects, tag links with colors, and search across everything — titles, URLs, tags, even the pages you've visited.

**Illustration:** A split view mockup — left side showing a folder tree sidebar, right side showing bookmark cards with colorful tag pills. A magnifying glass hovers over the content. Warm wood-toned sidebar, white content cards.

**CTA Button:** "Next →"

---

## Screen 4 — You're All Set

**Headline:** Start saving.

**Body:** Press `⌘B` anywhere to save the page you're on. Or click the Plank icon in your Dock to browse your library.

**Illustration:** A stylized keyboard with the `⌘` and `B` keys highlighted in primary blue. A small bookmark icon floats above the keys. Confetti-like subtle dots in the background. Celebratory but not overwhelming.

**CTA Button:** "Open Plank"
